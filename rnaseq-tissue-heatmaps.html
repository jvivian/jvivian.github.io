RNA-Seq Expression Heatmaps

Average Euclidean Distance between Tissues


Each heatmap value is the median euclidean distance of all combinations of points between all samples for two tissues. The code to produce these plots is located here. The left-hand plots used all protein-coding genes (~20k genes), and the right-hand plots used a small subset of 409 oncogenes curated by UCSF.
